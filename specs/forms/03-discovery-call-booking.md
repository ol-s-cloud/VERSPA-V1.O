# Form: Discovery Call Booking

**Used on:** book-a-call.html
**HubSpot form name:** VERSPA — Book a Discovery Call
**Redirect after submit:** /thank-you-discovery-call
**Note:** If HubSpot Meetings is available, embed the calendar directly. If not, use this form and follow up manually.

## Fields

| # | Field | Type | Required | Options |
|---|-------|------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Full name | Text | Yes | — |
| 2 | Email address | Email | Yes | — |
| 3 | I am a | Dropdown | Yes | Project developer / Renewable energy investor / Institutional investor / DFI or development bank / Advisor or consultant / Press or media / Other |
| 4 | What would you like to discuss? | Textarea | No | — |
| 5 | Preferred time zone | Dropdown | No | GMT / GMT+1 / GMT+2 / GMT+3 / WAT (West Africa) / EAT (East Africa) / GST (Gulf) / EST / Other |

## CRM pipeline
- Contact type: determined by 'I am a' field
- Deal stage: Discovery Call Requested
- Priority: highest urgency — follow up within 24 hours
