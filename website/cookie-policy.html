What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences, and help us understand how our site is used.

How to manage cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of this website. For instructions, visit allaboutcookies.org.
