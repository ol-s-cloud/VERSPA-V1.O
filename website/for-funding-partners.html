Target returns

Financial returns.
Real-world impact.

VERSPA targets competitive returns across all investment instruments — while every pound invested directly funds renewable energy generation and CO₂ reduction. You do not have to choose between returns and impact.

Debt instruments
5–7%
Target annual return
Fixed target return paid periodically. Capital returned at maturity. Lower risk profile. Suitable for retail investors seeking predictable income from renewable energy projects.
Equity instruments
7–9%+
Target annual return
Variable return with higher upside potential. Investors participate in project revenues. Higher risk and reward profile. Suitable for investors with longer time horizons and higher risk appetite.
Blended finance
6–8%
Target annual return
Combination of debt and equity components, often with grant funding stacked alongside. Available on selected projects. Suitable for institutional and impact-focused investors seeking structured exposure.

All investments carry risk, including the risk of capital loss. Target returns are not guaranteed and are based on comparable platforms — including Enerfip, which has delivered an average net IRR of approximately 6.3% — and are not indicative of future performance on VERSPA. Investments in renewable energy projects are illiquid and typically long-duration. VERSPA is in the process of seeking FCA authorisation.
