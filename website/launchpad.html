What is the Launchpad

The gap between a project
and its funding.

Most renewable energy projects fail to reach financial close not because they are bad projects — but because they are not investment-ready. The documentation is incomplete, the compliance pathway is unclear, and the right capital cannot find them.

The VERSPA Launchpad closes that gap. In 12 weeks, we take your project through a structured investment readiness process — producing the commercial feasibility report, identifying and applying for relevant grants, structuring the deal documentation, and preparing your project for listing on the VERSPA Capital Marketplace once FCA authorisation is received.

Advisory services are live now and do not require FCA authorisation. You can engage VERSPA today.

Programme phases

Three phases.
One outcome.

The Launchpad is structured in three sequential phases, each building on the last. By the end of week 12, your project is investment-ready.

1
Phase 1
WEEKS 1–4

Assess & diagnose

We start by understanding your project completely — its stage, its gaps, its potential and its risks. This phase produces a clear picture of what your project needs to reach financial close and a structured plan to get there.

Project assessment and scoping call
Commercial feasibility report (CFR) production
Grant radar scan — all open funding calls matched to your project
Gap analysis — what is missing from your investment case
2
Phase 2
WEEKS 5–9

Structure & document

With the diagnosis complete, we structure your deal and build the documentation package that investors and institutions require. This phase turns your project from an idea into a structured investment opportunity.

Investment structure design — debt, equity or blended
Term sheet and investment agreement templates
SPV setup guidance where required
Grant applications submitted with our support

Phase 3

Cross-border compliance confirmation
KYC/AML documentation package
Investor pitch preparation and review
Investment readiness sign-off and marketplace listing preparation
What you get

Six deliverables.
One investment-ready project.

Commercial Feasibility Report
A full CFR covering market analysis, financial modelling, risk assessment and commercial viability — the foundational document every investor and grant body requires.
Grant Identification & Applications
A full scan of 120+ grant sources across Innovate UK, UKRI, EU Green Deal, AfDB, IFC and BII — matched to your project type, country and stage. Applications submitted with our support.
Deal Structure & Term Sheet
Investment structure design — debt, equity, grant or blended finance — with a fully drafted term sheet and investment agreement template tailored to your project and jurisdiction.
Compliance & Regulatory Pathway
Cross-border compliance mapping across your project's jurisdiction — regulatory requirements, FCA pathway where applicable, KYC/AML documentation package and SPV setup guidance.
VERSPA Intelligence Scoring
Your project is scored across innovation, viability, market readiness and ESG impact using the VERSPA Intelligence engine — the same scoring investors and institutions use to evaluate projects on the platform.
Marketplace Listing Preparation
Your project is prepared for listing on the VERSPA Capital Marketplace — investor pitch, project summary, impact metrics and all documentation — ready for the moment FCA authorisation is received.

Who it's for

Built for serious
project developers.

The Launchpad is designed for renewable energy project developers who have a real project and the ambition to raise capital — but need structured support to get investment-ready.

Early-stage developers with a project site or concept
You have a site, a project idea and a plan — but you need the commercial feasibility report, deal structure and compliance pathway to make it investable. The Launchpad builds all of this for you.
Developers ready to structure and raise
Your project is in development and you are ready to approach investors — but you need your documentation and deal structure in order first. The Launchpad accelerates the final steps to investment readiness.
Projects seeking grants alongside equity or debt
You want to blend grant funding with private capital — but navigating Innovate UK, AfDB, IFC and EU Green Deal simultaneously is complex. The Launchpad manages all of this in parallel.
UK, EU and EMEA projects — all geographies
Whether your project is in Scotland, Nigeria, Kenya or the UAE — the Launchpad is designed for cross-border renewable energy finance. We have compliance coverage across all VERSPA target jurisdictions.
