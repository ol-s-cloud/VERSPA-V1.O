Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · VERSPA Ltd · London, United Kingdom

1. Who we are

VERSPA Ltd (“VERSPA”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is a company registered in England and Wales, headquartered in London, United Kingdom. We operate the website verspa.co.uk and related services. VERSPA is the data controller for the personal data we collect.

2. What data we collect

We collect personal data that you provide directly to us through our website forms, including:

  • Identity data: first name, last name, company or project name
  • Contact data: email address, phone number (where provided)
  • Professional data: investor type, investment range, project type, project stage, country of residence or project location
  • Communication data: messages, enquiry content, discussion notes from discovery calls
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time on site (collected via cookies)
  • Marketing data: how you heard about VERSPA, geography preferences

3. Why we collect it and our legal basis

We collect and process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your enquiries and manage your registration — legal basis: legitimate interest and contract performance
  • To provide advisory services including the Launchpad programme — legal basis: contract performance
  • To send you platform updates, regulatory progress and relevant project information — legal basis: consent (you may withdraw at any time)
  • To segment and route communications based on your investor or developer type — legal basis: legitimate interest
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations — legal basis: legal obligation

4. How long we keep your data

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected. For waitlist registrations, we retain data until the platform launches and for a period of 2 years thereafter. For Launchpad clients, we retain data for 7 years following completion of services. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data with:

  • HubSpot Inc. — our CRM and marketing automation platform, acting as a data processor on our behalf
  • Professional advisors including legal, compliance and accounting professionals, under confidentiality obligations
  • Regulatory authorities where required by law

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website and to understand how our site is used. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

7. Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK data subject, you have the right to: access your personal data; correct inaccurate data; request erasure of your data; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@verspa.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

8. International transfers

Your data may be processed outside the UK by HubSpot, which operates servers in the United States. HubSpot participates in the UK-US Data Bridge framework. By submitting your data, you acknowledge this transfer.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. The date of the most recent update is shown at the top of this page.

10. Contact us

For any questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@verspa.co.uk or via our contact form.
