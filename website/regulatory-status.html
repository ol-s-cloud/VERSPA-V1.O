Regulatory status
⚠️ Advisory services only — FCA authorisation in progress

What we can do.
What we cannot. Yet.

VERSPA is committed to full transparency on our regulatory position. This page tells you exactly what VERSPA can and cannot currently do — and where we are on the path to full FCA authorisation.

What we can do now — no FCA licence required
Grant advisory — identifying and applying for Innovate UK, UKRI, AfDB, IFC and EU Green Deal funding
Commercial Feasibility Report (CFR) production for renewable energy projects
Deal structuring advisory — term sheets, investment structure design, SPV guidance
Compliance and regulatory pathway advisory across UK, EU and EMEA jurisdictions
The VERSPA Launchpad — 12-week investment readiness programme (advisory only)
VERSPA Intelligence project scoring and grant radar
Investor and developer waitlist registration and information provision
Educational content, market intelligence and platform updates
What we cannot do yet — requires FCA authorisation
Facilitate investment transactions between investors and project developers
Hold client money or investor funds
List investment opportunities on a regulated marketplace
Operate a peer-to-peer lending or equity crowdfunding platform
Make financial promotions to retail investors
Provide regulated financial advice or investment recommendations
FCA authorisation pathway

Our path to full
platform authorisation.

We are pursuing FCA authorisation as a crowdfunding platform operator under the Financial Services and Markets Act 2000. This is the most rigorous and globally respected regulatory seal in alternative finance.
